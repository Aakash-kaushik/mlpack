\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The Cauchy kernel. 

Given two vector $ x $, $ y $, and a bandwidth $ \sigma $ (set in the constructor),

\[ K(x, y) = \frac{1}{1 + (\frac{|| x - y ||}{\sigma})^2}. \]
