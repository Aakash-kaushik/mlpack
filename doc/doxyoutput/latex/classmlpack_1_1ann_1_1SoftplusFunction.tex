The softplus function, defined by. 

\begin{eqnarray*} f(x) &=& \ln(1 + e^{x}) \\ f\textnormal{\textquotesingle}(x) &=& \frac{1}{1 + e^{-x}} \\ f^{-1}(y) &=& \ln(e^{y} - 1) \end{eqnarray*}
